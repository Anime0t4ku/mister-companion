Privacy Policy for MiSTer Companion Mobile

Effective date: April 13, 2026

MiSTer Companion Mobile respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information the app handles

MiSTer Companion Mobile may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved device names
  • MiSTer IP addresses or hostnames
  • Usernames
  • Passwords
  • App preferences and configuration settings

This information is used only to let the app connect to and manage your MiSTer device.

How your information is used

The app uses the information you enter to:

  • Connect to your MiSTer device over your network
  • Save your device list and preferences locally on your phone
  • Perform actions you request, such as reading device status or sending management commands

Data sharing

MiSTer Companion Mobile does not sell your personal data.

MiSTer Companion Mobile does not share your personal data with third parties.

Data collection

MiSTer Companion Mobile does not collect personal data on external servers operated by the developer.

Any connection information you enter is stored locally on your device and used only for the app’s functionality.

Network communication

The app connects directly to the MiSTer device you configure. This communication is initiated by you and is used only to provide the app’s features.

Data retention and deletion

Stored connection details and settings remain on your device until you remove them, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

You can delete your stored information at any time by:

  • Removing saved devices inside the app
  • Clearing the app’s storage in Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Security

The app is designed to use your saved connection details only for connecting to your MiSTer device. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Children’s privacy

MiSTer Companion Mobile is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy text with a new effective date.
